TECHNICAL TALKS

Date: 05 Dec 2019

Time: 9 AM - 4 PM

Event Category: Technical Talks

Speakers:
Dr. C.V.K. BHANU ,Dean Academic Program (PG and Research) on SMART GRIDS
Principal, Dr. A.B. Koteswara Rao on ROBOT- DESIGN ISSUES

Introduction

IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A) is organising TECHNICAL TALKS on December 5th at the Mini Auditorium that refreshes your mind and encompasses ideas by knowing the contemporary world around you.
The session will be started by the addressing of Dr.S.LakshmiNarayana past IEEE Vizag Bay Sub Sub Section Chair.
The morning session will be followed by the technical talk Delivered by Dr. C.V.K. BHANU ,Dean Academic Program (PG and Research), Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering (Autonomous) on SMART GRIDS.
The afternoon session will be continued by our very own beloved Principal, Dr. A.B. Koteswara Rao on ROBOT- DESIGN ISSUES which is followed by the visit to CAD/ROBOTICS lab visit in our college.
Nothing is more expensive than missed opportunity.
